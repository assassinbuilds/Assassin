# Brand Guide

## Name

Tech Assassin

## Tagline

Learn. Build. Ship. Repeat.

## Public Meaning

At Tech Assassin, Assassin means precision, focus, discipline, and execution. It is not about violence.

A Tech Assassin removes confusion, beats procrastination, builds with discipline, and ships real work.

## ASSASSIN

- Ambitious
- Skilled
- Strategic
- Adaptive
- Squad-Based
- Shipping
- Innovative
- Next-Gen

## Voice

The brand should feel:

- Practical
- Student-first
- Energetic
- Professional enough for colleges, sponsors, and collaborators
- Focused on proof and shipping

Avoid language that sounds violent, threatening, or overly aggressive in public copy.

## Visual Direction

Keep the current white-background UI direction:

- Background: white or near-white
- Text: slate or black
- Accent: red
- Cards: white with soft borders
- Buttons: black primary with red hover/accent
